    1. `pub fn calculate_daily_capacity(work_hours: &WorkHoursConfig, events_by_date: &HashMap<NaiveDate, Vec<CalendarEvent>>, from: NaiveDate, through: NaiveDate) -> Vec<(NaiveDate, i32)>`
       - Iterate each date from `from` through `through` (inclusive)
       - Skip dates whose weekday name (lowercase 3-letter: "mon", "tue"...) is NOT in `work_hours.work_days`

       - Sum `duration_minutes` of returned OpenBlocks as that day's capacity
       - Return vec of (date, capacity_minutes) tuples

         - Otherwise: sum capacity from daily_capacity for dates where date >= today AND date <= due_date. If estimated_minutes > total_capacity -> DeadlineRisk::AtRisk
       - Sort results by severity (Critical first, then Warning, then Info), then by days_remaining ascending
